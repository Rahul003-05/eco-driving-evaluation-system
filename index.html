About The Project

Title: Eco-Driving Level Evaluation Model for Electric Buses Entering and Leaving Stops.

Electric Buses play a vital role in sustainable urban transportation, but their energy efficiency is highly influenced by driving behavior during frequent stop entries and departures.

This system uses Machine Learning (XGBoost & Gradient Boosting) alongside Explainable AI to evaluate real-time telemetry data. It provides instant feedback and dynamic dashboards to help fleet managers optimize battery life and improve passenger comfort.


Developed By: Rahul, Wasiya, and Chandana
